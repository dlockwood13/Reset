Get ready
Cycle 0 of 4

5-4-3-2-1 grounding

Bring yourself back to the present moment. Take your time with each one.

5
things you can see
Look around. Name them quietly.
4
things you can feel
Your feet on the floor, fabric on skin.
3
things you can hear
Traffic, a clock, your own breath.
2
things you can smell
Or two scents you like.
1
thing you can taste
Or one thing you're grateful for today.
You are more than this moment.
